VR Kitchen Experiment

Participant Instructions & Mode Reference  ·  The Inputters  ·  Spring 2026

Overview

In this experiment you will work in a virtual reality kitchen to prepare and serve food orders to customers. There are three food items: Coffee, Burger, and Pizza. Each customer will be waiting for one of these items — prepare it correctly and serve it before their timer runs out.

Depending on the input mode assigned to you, you will interact using physical controllers, hand tracking, voice commands, or a combination of these.

Note for experimenters: Select the participant's assigned mode from the dropdown on the start screen before handing over the headset. The mode determines which input methods are available in the kitchen.

Starting the Experiment

  1. Put on the VR headset and wait for the start screen to appear.
  2. The experimenter will select the correct input mode from the dropdown menu.
  3. Press Start. The kitchen scene will load.
  4. If your mode includes voice commands, a help panel will appear automatically summarising the available commands. It will close after 15 seconds, or say "close" to dismiss it early.
  5. Customers will appear at the serving counter. Prepare and serve their orders.
  6. When finished, use the Finish button (or say "finish" in Voice-only mode) to end the session.

Input Modes

CONTROLLERS

Controller

Full physical interaction using VR controllers. Grab, place, and interact with all kitchen objects manually. No voice.

HANDS

Hands

Full physical interaction using hand tracking. Pinch and grab objects naturally. No voice.

VOICE

Voice Only

All kitchen actions are performed entirely by voice command. Full command set available.

CONTROLLERSVOICE

Controllers + Voice

Controllers handle all preparation. Voice is limited to serving customers only.

HANDSVOICE

Hands + Voice

Hands handle food assembly. Voice controls machines (grill, oven, coffee maker) and serving.

TEST

Test (All)

All input methods enabled simultaneously. Used for testing and calibration only.

Tip: In any mode that includes voice, hold the A button on the right controller while speaking, then release. The display will show "Listening…" while recording and confirm the recognised command when successful. Say "help coffee", "help burger", or "help pizza" at any time for a quick in-game command reference.

Food Preparation Summary

☕ Coffee

  1. Start the coffee maker (machine button or voice).
  2. Get a cup (spawn button or voice).
  3. Pour coffee into the cup (pour button or voice).
  4. Serve the filled cup to the correct customer.

🍔 Burger

  1. Place a patty on the grill.
  2. Turn on the grill and wait for the patty to cook.
  3. Place the bottom bun on the assembly plate.
  4. Transfer the cooked patty to the burger.
  5. Add desired toppings (cheese, tomato, onion, lettuce, bacon).
  6. Add the top bun to complete the burger.
  7. Serve to the correct customer.

🍕 Pizza

  1. Place the pizza dough on the assembly board.
  2. Add toppings (pepperoni, mushroom, olive, pepper, basil).
  3. Put the pizza in the oven.
  4. Turn on the oven and wait for it to cook.
  5. Serve to the correct customer.
Customers: Each customer displays their number above their head (Customer 1, 2, 3). Match the food type to the right customer when serving — serving the wrong food will not count.

General Tips

  • Each customer's number is displayed above their head to help with voice serving commands.
  • The grill and oven can burn food if left on too long — watch for visual and notification cues.
  • The trash can will destroy any object placed inside it.
  • If you forget a voice command, say "help coffee", "help burger", or "help pizza" for a quick reminder panel.
  • Voice recognition works best with clear, calm speech at a normal pace.
  • "Patty" may occasionally be recognised as "paddy" — both are accepted.
